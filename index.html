Data Mining and Warehousing

Data Mining and Warehousing and its applications.

Course Overview

This course is an introduction to the fundamental concepts of data mining including motivations for, and applications of, data mining. The course provides an overview of the ethics and privacy issues with respect to invasive use, and the exploration of data mining technologies. This course also introduces students to the techniques used in mining information from the web including logs, forms, cookies, user identification, and path analysis.

Course Logistics and Policies

Grade There will be eight quizzes during the course, making up 20% of the final grade. Students will also complete eight in-class activities, which count for another 20% and are designed to support learning through participation. The project and presentation will make up 40% of the grade, giving students a chance to explore a topic and show what they have learned. The final exam will cover all course material and will count for the remaining 20% of the grade.
